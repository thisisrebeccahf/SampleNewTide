What is A New Tide ?

A New Tide is an organization of youth ages 15-21 focusing on flipping the 25th district in California. We focus primarily on promoting people to register and pre-register to vote, adopting for Katie Hill, and engaging and educating youth on their communities.

Why We Fight

To know what A New Tide is, you have to know why we're here. Why We Fight is a series of videos that we've put together to talk about issues that are important to us-- issues like ending environmental racism, securing gun control, bringing about economic equality, and more. As you watch the series, we hope that you'll think about your reason for fighting, too.
